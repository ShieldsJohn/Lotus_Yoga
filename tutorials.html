Beginner

Hero pose (Virasana)

• Start by kneeling on the floor with your knees together and your feet slightly wider than hip-width apart. • Point your toes backward and sit on your heels. • Keep your spine straight and shoulders relaxed. • You can place your hands on your thighs or in your lap. • Hold the pose for a few breaths, gradually increasing the time as you become more comfortable.

Child's pose (Balasana)

• Begin on your hands and knees in a tabletop position. • Sit back onto your heels, keeping your big toes together and knees apart. • Extend your arms forward on the mat and lower your forehead to the ground. • Relax your entire body, focusing on deep breaths.

Extended puppy pose (Uttana Shishosana)

• Start in a tabletop position on your hands and knees. • Walk your hands forward while lowering your chest toward the mat. • Keep your hips stacked over your knees. • Stretch your arms and lower your forehead to the ground. • Lengthen your spine, feeling a stretch in your upper body.

Intermediate

Bridge pose (Setu Bandhasana)

• Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet hip-width apart, flat on the floor. • Place your arms alongside your body, palms facing down. • Press through your feet and lift your hips off the ground. • Roll your shoulders underneath you and clasp your hands together if possible. • Keep your thighs parallel to each other and engage your glutes. • Hold for a few breaths and then gently release.
